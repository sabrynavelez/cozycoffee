We Are
Brewed Awakening

Comfort. Community. Coffee.

We are Seattle’s coziest hideaway for coffee lovers, dreamers, and change-makers! Nestled in the heart of Seattle, we’re more than just a coffee shop — we’re a community hub where good coffee meets good conversation.

Brewed Awakening was born out of a passion for quality coffee, environmental consciousness, and a commitment to make every cup a catalyst for connection and change. Our story started with a group of friends who wanted a space where everyone felt at home and inspired, whether they were stopping in for their morning brew, settling in to work, or just looking to recharge with good company.

Our café reflects Seattle’s green, progressive spirit — you’ll find recycled wood tables, lush plants cascading from the walls, and earthy tones that create a vibe as warm as our lattes. From ethically sourced beans roasted by local roasters to compostable packaging and eco-friendly practices, everything we do is rooted in sustainability and a love for our planet.

We’re proud to be a welcoming space where every voice is valued and celebrated. Step into Brewed Awakening for a handcrafted drink, stay for the cozy, creative ambiance, and become a part of our growing community. Whether you're here to recharge, reconnect, or rethink over a cup of our rich, perfectly brewed coffee, we can't wait to welcome you.

Our Coffee & Sourcing

We take great pride in the coffee we serve. Our beans are sourced from the best growers around the world, ensuring quality and sustainability in every cup. We believe in transparency and building lasting relationships with our farmers to ensure ethical and fair trade practices. Each batch is carefully roasted by local artisans who bring out the unique flavors and aromas of our beans. We focus on single-origin coffees, giving you a taste of the distinct regions where our beans are grown.

At Brewed Awakening, we’re committed to more than just great coffee — we’re dedicated to the journey each bean takes from farm to cup. Our beans are hand-selected from small, sustainable farms around the world, with a focus on supporting farmers who prioritize environmental stewardship and fair wages. From the rich soils of Ethiopia to the highlands of Colombia, every origin tells a story, and we’re honored to share those stories through the unique, flavorful coffees we serve.
